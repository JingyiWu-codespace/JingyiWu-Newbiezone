Forty years of GTI!

Home -> Everything about Volkswagen -> Volkswagen Magazine -> 40 years of GTI.

The new up! To date, GTI is the last realization to feature the famous logo that appeared 42
years ago. With the Polo and the Golf, it is also the third model in the current range that uses
the time-honored GTI success formula. Previously this only happened twice.

All GTIs produced since the introduction of the mythical three capital letters have the same qualities in common: perfectly balanced, dynamic driving thanks to low weight, a spirited drive and a taut, yet comfortable suspension. The GTI models guarantee the driver extraordinary driving sensations behind the sports steering wheel that fits perfectly in the hand.

GTI models thus guarantee the
driver extraordinary driving sensations.

Volkswagen

The range today consists of the up! GTI, the Polo GTI and the Golf GTI. A triple offering that has only been available three times before in Volkswagen 's history . The last time was between 2000 and 2005 with the Lupo GTI, the Polo GTI and the Golf GTI. In 1977, three models carried the three letters on the radiator grille: the Golf, the Scirocco and the Passat. The latter only had this privilege in the concept phase, because the series version was given the name GLI in 1979.
